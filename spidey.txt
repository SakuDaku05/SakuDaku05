🕷️ Key Elements to Copy from the Pokémon Example
Custom Banner/Header

They used a themed banner at the top.
→ You can design a Spider‑Man inspired banner (red/blue gradient, web overlays, comic font) and upload it to your repo.

Badges & Shields

They added badges with custom colors and logos.
→ Use shields.io to make Spider‑Man quotes or fun phrases:

markdown
![Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/With_Great_Power-red?style=for-the-badge)
![Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Come_Swing_With_Me-blue?style=for-the-badge)
Playful Emojis

Pokémon emojis were sprinkled throughout.
→ Use 🕷️ 🕸️ 🦸‍♂️ for Spider‑Man flavor.

Stats Cards

They embedded GitHub stats and streak cards.
→ You can theme them with red/blue:

markdown
![Stats](https://github-readme-stats.vercel.app/api?username=saketkumarsingh&theme=radical)
Sections with Personality

Pokémon example had “Trainer Card” style sections.
→ You can make “🕸️ Web‑Slinger Projects” or “🕷️ Villains I’ve Defeated (bugs fixed)” sections.
